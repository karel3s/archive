Approach

Our web design process

1. Goal identification: Working closely with our clients, we determine the objectives that the new website should achieve, clarifying its purpose.

2. Scope definition: Once the website's goals are established, we outline the project's scope. This includes identifying the necessary web pages and features required to fulfill those goals, as well as establishing a timeline for their development.

3. Sitemap and wireframe creation: With a clear understanding of the project's scope, we proceed to create a sitemap, outlining how the defined content and features will interact with each other.

4. Content creation: With a holistic view of the website, we begin crafting content for individual pages while maintaining a focus on search engine optimization (SEO). It is essential to have genuine content at this stage.

5. Visual elements: Building upon the site's structure and content, we delve into the visual branding. Depending on the client's preferences, this may involve refining an existing visual style or creating a brand-new one. Tools such as style tiles, moodboards, and element collages facilitate this process.

6. Testing: At this point, all pages have been developed, and their display to site visitors is determined. To ensure a seamless user experience, we conduct thorough testing. This includes manual browsing across various devices and employing automated site crawlers to identify issues ranging from user experience glitches to broken links.

7. Launch: Once everything is functioning seamlessly, it's time to plan and execute the launch of your website. This involves carefully deciding when to launch and how to effectively communicate the news to the world. After that, it's time to celebrate and enjoy the achievement!
